What Juxta Offers

Compare Companies

Whether it's exploring what companies are out there or trying to decide on a company to work for, it can be difficult to efficiently compare companies. Although there is a lot of information out there about them, there is no centralized place to view that information. Our solution allows you to:

  • search for companies
  • add/remove them to a comparison list
  • visually compare those companies against each other and against the industry average using radar charts

Industry Statistics

For your chosen industry, Juxta shows you relevant visual information to help you have a better picture of what that industry has to offer and its trends, allowing you to make a more informed decision of which industry to pursue or to simple know more about your own industry. You will be able to:

  • compare salaries for last 5 years
  • compare top 5 paid positions trending
  • compare positions' salaries vs. income
